Favorite photography style - Japanese film
The picture on the left is a digital-to-film photo I took for my mother. The biggest feature of film is graininess. Perhaps the most salient quality of the film grain is motion, the grain is always moving, swimming, which means that even a still object, like a flower on a table, that flower is alive even if it's not moving. This is why I like Chinese film most.This graininess gives the whole picture a sense of breath and movement .

The beauty of photography is the perceptual display of ideas
There is such a photographer in the annals of photography, his name is Man Ray. Living in the 20th century, Man Ray was deeply influenced by the Dada school and surrealism at that time, and the content he shot was full of dreamlike feeling. Man Ray had profound painting skills and had many influences on photography techniques. Moreover, he met a woman named Gigi who cooperated with him and became a legend in the history of photography. He once said, "For me, photography is just a means to expression, not an end." This is the concept of aesthetics, and we apply it to our photographic practice through our own cognition of beauty itself, beauty as a form, beauty as a feeling, etc. This is the essence of photography aesthetics, that kind of ideological beauty. Take Man Ray's book "A Girl and Five Tears" for example, to show the idea he wants to express with photographic techniques is the emotional display of the idea. In the fixed "boundary" of the artistic atmosphere in the society at that time, he could easily break through this "boundary", which is precisely his deep understanding and application of photography and aesthetics .

Favorite Dance style - Urban Dance
Urban Dance has been called a "work of art" and holds a special place in the hearts of many dancers for its extraordinary creativity, quality choreography and melodrama . Urban Dance is not a kind of dance, but a form of dance expression. It is a kind of individual expression that does not adhere to tradition. It is a dancer's pursuit of art and an organic integration of trends, new elements and dance.

Chinese folk dance
Singing and dancing, free and lively. One of the main characteristics of Chinese folk dance is the close combination of dance and singing. This form of singing and dancing is free, vivid and lively, and can easily express more life content than pure dance, and it is easy to understand, so it is very popular with the broad masses of Chinese people. Skillful use of props, skill combination. Many Chinese folk dances skillfully use props, such as fans, handkerchiefs, long silk, tabla, single drum, flower stick, lantern, flower umbrella, etc., which greatly strengthens the artistic expression of the dance and makes the dance movements more rich, beautiful and colorful. The plot is vivid and the image is vivid. Chinese folk dances focus on the content, most of which are based on certain stories and legends. Therefore, the characters have distinct images and prominent personalities.

Favorite tea - black tea
Black tea is the young leaves or buds of tea in the camellia family. Black tea is not naturally grown, but was made on the basis of green tea in the late Ming and early Qing Dynasties. It is suitable for the tea tree new teeth leaves as raw materials, after withering, rolling (cutting), fermentation, drying and a series of processes refined tea, fermentation makes the ingredients in the tea produce chemical reactions, brewing with water, not only aroma to welcome people, but also show a unique dark red tea color, so it is called "black tea". Black tea contains vitamins, caffeine, amino acids, minerals, polysaccharides, tea polyphenols and other nutritional and functional components.
